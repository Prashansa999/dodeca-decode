How to play

  1. Each day hides one answer tied to today's date, a person, an event, or a thing.
  2. The board has 12 tiles, all closed. Tap any tile to reveal a short clue.
  3. You can guess at any time. Spelling is forgiving; just get the gist.
  4. You start at 100 points. Every tile you open costs -8, and each wrong guess costs -10.
  5. Open as few tiles as you can, then share your score and keep your 🔥 streak alive.
Goal: name it with the fewest tiles open. The fewer you reveal, the higher your score.
